# Fleeting Notes

This is your space for capturing thoughts, ideas, and observations as they come to you. Don't worry about structure or polish — just get it down before it slips away.

## How to use this folder

- **Create a new note** using the `Fleeting Note` template whenever a thought strikes
- **Keep it short** — a few sentences is perfectly fine
- **Review periodically** — revisit your fleeting notes and promote the good ones into full knowledge notes in your other folders

> "The best time to capture an idea is the moment it arrives."
